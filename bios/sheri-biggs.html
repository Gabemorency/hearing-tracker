Sheri Biggs
United States House of Representatives
District 3 · South Carolina · Republican
Biography

Sheri Biggs is a South Carolina native who built her career as a veterinarian before transitioning into public service, earning her Doctor of Veterinary Medicine degree and operating a veterinary practice in the Upstate region of the state. Her background in small business ownership and healthcare shaped her perspective on economic and regulatory issues that would later define her political priorities.

Biggs was elected to represent South Carolina's 3rd Congressional District in 2024, succeeding Jeff Duncan and continuing the district's tradition of conservative representation. She has aligned herself with Republican efforts to reduce federal spending, strengthen border security, and support agricultural and rural communities that form the backbone of her district. Her committee assignments and early legislative focus have reflected both her professional expertise in veterinary medicine and her constituents' interests in farming, small business, and Second Amendment rights.

Serving in the 119th Congress, Biggs continues to advocate for the rural and working-class communities of western South Carolina, which include the Upstate and portions of the state's agricultural heartland. She has prioritized issues such as fiscal responsibility, energy independence, and protecting the livelihoods of farmers and small business owners in her district. Her background as a practicing veterinarian and entrepreneur lends a practical, constituents-first approach to her legislative work in Washington.
